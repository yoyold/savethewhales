Why Save the Whales?

Whales play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of marine ecosystems. By protecting them, we are safeguarding the health of our oceans and the planet as a whole. These magnificent creatures help regulate the ocean's carbon cycle, support biodiversity, and inspire wonder in people around the world.
