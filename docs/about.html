About Save the Whales

Learn more about our mission and the importance of saving whales.

Our Mission

Save the Whales is dedicated to the conservation and protection of whale populations worldwide. We work to raise awareness about the threats facing these magnificent creatures and promote sustainable practices that protect marine ecosystems.

Why Whales Matter

Whales are keystone species in marine ecosystems. They help maintain the health of our oceans by:

  • Supporting the marine food web through nutrient cycling
  • Promoting phytoplankton growth, which produces oxygen and captures carbon
  • Maintaining biodiversity in ocean environments
  • Serving as indicators of ocean health

The Threats They Face

Despite international protection efforts, whales continue to face numerous threats including ship strikes, entanglement in fishing gear, ocean noise pollution, climate change, and habitat degradation. Through education, advocacy, and conservation action, we can help protect these incredible animals for future generations.
